Funsitan ang buong pamilya ng pansit

More than a noodle house — a nationwide pansit journey. Kwatogs is on its way to becoming the pansit capital of the Philippines, bringing the country's best-loved regional specialties together under one roof — from Pansit Batil-Patong ng Tuguegarao and Pansit Habhab ng Lucban to Pansit Malabon, Palabok, Japchae, at marami pang darating.
